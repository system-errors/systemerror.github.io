2. Data We Collect

To enhance your experience on our website and gather insights into website usage, we use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly web analytics tool. GoatCounter does not use cookies and does not track personal data. The information collected is entirely anonymous and includes the following:

  • Country: The country from which you access our site.

  • Operating System: The operating system of your device (e.g., Windows, macOS, Linux).
  • Screen Size: The dimensions of your screen, which helps us optimize the user experience.
  • Language Settings: The language preferences set in your browser or device.

3. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to analyze the general usage of our website and improve user experience. We do not use this data to identify or track individual users. It helps us understand how our site is used, so we can make improvements.

GoatCounter retains anonymous usage data for a limited time to perform analysis. No personally identifiable information is retained.
